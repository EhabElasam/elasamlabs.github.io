Frequently Asked Questions

What is Elasam Labs?

Elasam Labs is an independent software brand focused on building practical apps, privacy-friendly tools, and AI-powered solutions for everyday users, students, families, and small businesses.

Are your apps really offline?

Most of our apps are designed to work offline whenever possible. This means you can use them without an internet connection, and your data stays on your device.

How is my data protected?

Privacy is our top priority. We follow industry best practices for data security and encryption. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Where can I find your apps?

Our apps are available on various platforms. Check the Apps section on our website for availability information.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues, please contact us at support@elasamlabs.com with a detailed description of the problem.

Do you collect user data?

We collect minimal data and only what is necessary to provide and improve our services. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete information.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please mark your email as urgent.
